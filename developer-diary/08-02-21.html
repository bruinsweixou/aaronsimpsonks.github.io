A New Headquarters

For the past year or so, I’ve been cultivating a list of cities in the United States I’d be interested in moving to. Kansas City is where I grew up and definitely a nice place, but for the type of career growth I desire, it’s much too small. What makes things tough is that I refuse to move to San Francisco, Los Angeles, New York, or London - coincidentally, the largest cities in the world with the most economic and tech activity. Those housing prices are way too high for me to ever justify, especially in a post-COVID world where remote work is becoming a possibility. That leaves a few candidates in the form of San Antonio, Dallas, Chicago (a personal favorite), Atlanta, and Denver. If I really had to bite the bullet and deal with high housing prices, I’d sooner go somewhere like Seattle, but that’s a big “if”. When it comes time to actually move, I’ll probably let the company that hires me dictate the final decision, but thoughts and recommendations are always welcome.
